About Me

I'm Ayomide Okeowo, a Data Scientist with a background in Finance and several years of hands on sales experience. I bring a unique blend of analytical expertise and commercial awareness, enabling me to develop data driven solutions that align with real business needs.

I hold a MSc in Applied Data Science from the University of Essex, where my dissertation focused on comparing ARIMA and LSTM models for forecasting on the FTSE 100. Prior to that, I earned a BSc in Finance and Management at the same university, where I was named to the Dean’s List and served as Vice President of the university’s boxing team.

My technical skills include Python, SQL, and tools such as Tableau and Power BI. I'm experienced in building predictive models, conducting A/B tests, and transforming raw datasets into actionable insights. I also work comfortably across cloud platforms like AWS and BigQuery, using libraries such as Scikit-learn, NLTK, and OpenCV. My sales background has sharpened my communication and stakeholder engagement skills, helping me bridge the gap between data science and decision-making. I am always seeking opportunities to learn, contribute, and grow.
